The CAP App Management team is responsible for reviewing apps to be included in the global library will require the following documents as a part of that process:

  1. Design Document
  2. Integration Test Report
  3. User Documentation Guidance These provide evidence that the app follows architectural, security, and QA guidelines. The board will use them in making a determination as to whether or not the app can be included in the global library.
